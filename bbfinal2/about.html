About BB Manufacturing
A decade of precision. A commitment to quality.
We don't just manufacture machines — we build the backbone of your business.

BB Manufacturing was founded with a simple mission: to provide Indian businesses with world-class soda fountain machines at honest, factory-direct prices.

Based in Surat, Gujarat, we have grown to become a trusted name in commercial soda machines. Our 10 models range from compact 4-tap units to flagship 20+3 tap machines capable of 10,000 glasses per day.

Every machine uses internationally sourced premium components — Roto Flow pumps from Italy, Lancer valves from Mexico, Syrup tanks from Hong Kong.
